The Curriculum
Eight Weeks. Zero to Confident.
Each week includes a private 1-hour session (in person or online), curated reading, and between-session support. The pace adapts to you.
WEEK 01
Why Blockchain Exists
The problem it solves. Bitcoin, Ethereum, and the shift from centralized to decentralized systems. Real examples from Africa, Latin America, and the Middle East.
WEEK 02
How Blockchain Works
Blocks, nodes, consensus. Public vs. private chains. Proof of Work vs. Proof of Stake. No code. Mental models only.
WEEK 03
The Blockchain Ecosystem
Major chains. Tokens vs. coins. Smart contracts, DeFi, NFTs, DAOs. Where AI agents are starting to operate on-chain and what that means for your industry.
AI + Blockchain
WEEK 04
Real-World Usage and Data
Adoption data by region. Trade finance, cross-border payments, land registries, supply chains. How AI is being used to verify and automate on-chain transactions in emerging markets.
AI + Blockchain
WEEK 05
RWAs and Tokenization
Real-world assets on-chain. Commodities, real estate, art. Regulatory landscape. AI-powered asset valuation and compliance tools entering the space.
AI + Blockchain
WEEK 06
Wallets and Safety
Hot vs. cold wallets. Hands-on setup. Seed phrases, private keys, recovery. Scam prevention. Security best practices.
WEEK 07
Exchanges and First Steps
CEX vs. DEX. KYC and onboarding. Your first purchase. Order types. Reading a basic chart. Fees and spreads.
WEEK 08
Your Blockchain Future
Personal strategy. Staking and yield basics. Portfolio mindset. Where blockchain and AI are heading next. A written roadmap you leave with. Ongoing access to my network.
